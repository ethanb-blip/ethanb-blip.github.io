Beginner Aquarium Guide

Building a healthy ecosystem for fish and plants

Introduction to Aquariums

An aquarium is a controlled ecosystem where fish, plants, and bacteria interact to create balance.

Nitrogen Cycle

Fish waste produces ammonia. Beneficial bacteria convert ammonia → nitrite → nitrate. Water changes remove nitrate and keep the environment healthy.

  • Ammonia – toxic
  • Nitrite – toxic
  • Nitrate – manageable

Filtration

Mechanical

Removes debris and particles.

Biological

Bacteria process ammonia and nitrite.

Chemical

Carbon or resins remove dissolved chemicals.
